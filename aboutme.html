Balayya Babu

Nandamuri Balakrishna, known as Balakrishna, is a dynamic Indian film actor and politician.[1][2] He appeared in more than 100 Telugu films over forty years in a variety of roles and established himself as one of the leading actors of Telugu cinema.[3] He won three Nandi Awards and one South Indian International Movie Award. He is an elected member of the Andhra Pradesh Legislative Assembly from Hindupur constituency since 2014.
